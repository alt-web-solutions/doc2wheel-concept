About Doc2Wheel

Experience, knowledge and a genuine passion for motorcycles.

Doc2Wheel is a motorcycle workshop based in Kerikeri, supporting riders with servicing, repairs, tyres, diagnostics, dyno tuning, parts, accessories and custom work.

The workshop has a strong focus on Harley Davidson and road bikes, helping local riders get practical advice and reliable support for their motorcycles.
